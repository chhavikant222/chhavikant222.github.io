Installation


For juicing function-

  • Place the auger into the drum.
  • Insert the juicing screen into the drum.
  • Place the end cap onto the drum end, and turn it counter-clockwise until it clicks firmly into place.

Working


  • Switch on the power botton under the juicer than switch on the power botton.
  • Now put the peeled fruits or veggies in the round hole on the top of the juicer.
  • Now rotate the switch clockwise to grind the fruits or veggies.
  • Now your fresh juice is ready for you.
