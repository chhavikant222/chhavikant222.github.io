Bhagat Singh

The young man who fought for his country.

Here's a timeline of Bhagat Singh:

  • 28 sep, 1907 - Born in the Banga, Pakistan.
  • 1916-1917, Education - Dayanand Anglo Vedic School.
  • 1923, Education - National College.
  • 1926 - Naujawan Bharat Sabha founded by him.
  • 1927 - Police concerned with Singh's influence on youths and arrested him.
  • 1928, Criminal charges - Murder of John P. Saunders and Channan Singh. And also responsible for the changing of name Hindustan Republican Association to Hindustan socialist Republican Association.
  • 1929 - He and Batukeshwar Dutt, set off two low intensity homemade bombs among some unoccupied benches of the Central Legislative Assembly.And he proposed a dramatic act to the HSRA intended to gain massive publicity for their aims.
  • 5 Oct, 1929 - Hunger strike started by Bhagat Singh with his cooperatives.
    • 23 March 1931 - Died, Lahore Central Jail.
